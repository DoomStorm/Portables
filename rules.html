Rules

There are few simple rules around here

  • DO NOT destroy other peoples constructions.
  • DO NOT steal from other players
  • DO NOT try to complicate others plans
  • DO NOT try to sabotage the server.
  • Exploiting bugs to overload the server is prohibited.
  • DO NOT spam plugin commands. This is considered as abuse and intentional server overload.

Breaking any rules and not respecting the fellow people, may give you permanent ban.

We aim for good behaivor, be it survival, pvp, events and construction. But do not expect that everyone wants to participate, so save everyones time. Respect a no!. Some players want to build their own constructions themselves, but feel free to ask, if you'd like to help somebody

/GIVE command is only for admins, and admins only. Admins can give you material if you ask nicely, but do not expect them to give all you need. (diamonds, gold, redstone and any other rare materials. Remember that giving users materials is easy and game loses its meaning. (Its all about gathering, neverending grinding and survival.
