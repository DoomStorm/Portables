About Minecraft

Minecraft is simple indie game developed by Notch. It is not free. But there are some cracked games on the net.

There is no goals in the game. Just sandboxing and building your own ideas. Gather material by mining, fishing and hunting. Chop some trees to get wood, pick up some flowers for decorations, mushroom for some soup and so on.

Though there are monesters hiding in dark corners and spawning in outside world during nights. So be prepared. Defend yourself and your constructs.

More information can be found on Minecraft official homepage.
